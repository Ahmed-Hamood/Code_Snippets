What is PLC ?

- What is PLC ?

PLC is short for programming logic controller, it consist of a computer with several additional hardware pieces that are a part to it or you can be attach to it at any time.

- Why PLC can we just use personal computer ?

PLC is an industrial grade computer device designed which is used for automating/controlling production machines. PLC is a ruggedized computer that has been designed to tolerate harsh amd noisy industrial environments. That is the main difference compared to personal computer.

- Where PLC is used ?

PLC is commonly used to control motors, relays, fans, lights and other equipments in factories and industrial operations. You can find them in automotive manufacture and plants, food and beverage factories, packaging machines. Here other places or manufacture industrials that our PLC utilized.

  • Car assembly
  • Oil and Gas
  • Power generation
  • minerals
  • Water processing
  • Cooling Systems
  • waste management
  • transportation

- What is the PLC purpose ?

PLC helps you to automate a sequence of process efficiently, and it provides a flexible way to connect different components together with an application software.

PLCs have a leaner operating system that is intended mainly for processing inputs and outputs based on programmed logic. That means PLC will react much faster which is essential for many applications.
