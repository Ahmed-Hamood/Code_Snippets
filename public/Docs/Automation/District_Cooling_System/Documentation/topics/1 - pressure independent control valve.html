Pressure Independent Control Valve

The pressure independent control valve is a device composed of an automatic flow rate regulator and a control valve with actuator.

PICV is a type of valve found in Hydronic systems, meaning water based, which provide heating and cooling in buildings.

The pressure independent control valve device can adjust flow rate and keep it constant in the presence of changing differential pressure conditions of the circuit in which it is installed.

PICV has two main functions:-

  • Control the amount of liquid flowing through a pipe
  • Automatically adjust and compensate for pressure fluctuations in the system to maintain a stable and reliable control.

These valves are basically a number of different valves conveniently combined into one unit. This saves on design and installation time as well as improving the efficiency of the system.
